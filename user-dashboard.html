Q.Why am I feeling dizziness and shortness of breath?

5 upvotes

There are several possible causes of shortness of breath and dizziness, such as asthma, stress, and anemia. Not all of these causes warrant a trip to the emergency room. An inefficient heart has a lower cardiac output, a lower amount of blood being sent out of the heart to the rest of the body with each beat. This leads to the feeling of shortness of breath.
