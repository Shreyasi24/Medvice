Who We Are

Our mission is to build a personalized, trusted
health knowledge repository

our values

User friendly

We want to provide an intuitive way for you to convey your problems and find the information that you need

Trust

We strive to make sure the experts on the platform are verified and the information on the platform is validated

Innovate responsibly

We know the importance of health information and work to protect all users on our platform

Empathy

Health can be a difficult journey for some and we want our content and communication to be thoughtful and supportive

the people

Zhichao Han

Co-Founder CEO

Zhichao have been building platforms for patients since 2020. Previously at PatientPartner, Zhichao build a patient connectivity platform from the ground up where prospective patients met past patients who have been through a similar health journey. While working with these patients, Zhichao discovered how hard it is for the everyday person to get the right guidance for their situation from someone they trust without booking an appointment. Zhichao saw an opportunity to connect people with trusted experts and organize the wealth of data into personalized information that we can all use
