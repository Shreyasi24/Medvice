How It Works

The Medvice Knowledgebase

Gain access to the world’s largest crowdsourced information repository created by trusted experts

Go through a guided workflow to post your question and health condition so that you get the right advice for your situation

Your monthly subscription goes towards compensating experts on the platform so that we can continue to build a large, qualified community of experts to help youś

02

Can’t find what you are looking for? Tell us what’s wrong

Are you feeling pain? Point out where it hurts, how intense the pain is and how long you have felt it. Not sure what something is on your skin? Post a picture. Have a specific question about a medication or a procedure? We can help with that as well.

03

Provide any additional relevant information

Similar to going to see a doctor, we ask a set of standard questions that help us provide the most relevant information for you. This information makes sure the advice is targeted to your health condition and our experts are well equipped to help you.
